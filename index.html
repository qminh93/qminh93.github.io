About me

Machine learning for biological sequence understanding

I am a computational biologist developing machine learning methods for biological sequence analysis. My research focuses on representation learning for protein and genomic sequences across multiple computational scales, from single-site analyses to large, collaborative learning settings. I design scalable and interpretable approaches for alignment, retrieval, and variant interpretation, with the goal of connecting sequence-level representations to biological structure, function, and phenotype.
